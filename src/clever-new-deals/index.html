new deal listeners

One of my favorite traditions at Clever is seeing how the new deal listeners have grown and developed over time. What started as a nice way to celebrate new business has continued and improved over the years. That said, as time has gone on sometimes listeners are lost or forgotten.

This site is meant as a archive of this bit of culture.
